Chicken Tetrazinni

Description

Chicken tetrazzini is a baked dish full of chicken and pasta goodness topped with cheese. It is a staple in my household, and will truly set you free. In other words, it will make your tongue slap your brains.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz linguini, cooked
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 4 chicken breasts, baked and diced
  • 2 cans cream of chicken soup w/ herbs
  • 2 8oz sour creams
  • 1 tsp. kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp. ground pepper
  • 1/2 cup chiken broth
  • 2 tbsp. parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella

Steps

    Cook noodles according to box, set aside. Combine 1/2 cup of butter, cooked chicken, soup, sour cream, salt, pepper and broth. Mix well and add in cooked noodles. Spray a 9x13 pan and pour in noodle mixture. Sprinkle cheese on top. Bake at 350 degrees for 40-45 min. Enjoy!
