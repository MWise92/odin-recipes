Buffalo Chicken Dip

Description

Buffalo Chicken Dip is a perfect appetizer for the big game or just a family get-together. It's a good balance of chicken and kick that will make your guests want the recipe from you.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. of boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 12oz. bottle of Frank's hot sauce
  • 1 8oz. container of cream cheese
  • 1 cup mexican cheese blend (or your favorite)
  • 1 cup ranch dressing
  • 1 bag torilla chips or veggies for dipping

Steps

    Poach about 1 lb. of boneless, skinless chicken in chicken stock. With forks, gently shred chicken into small chunks. In bowl, combine chicken with hot sauce to taste. Let the chicken soak in hot sauce until ready to use. Spread 8oz. of cream cheese in bottom of un-greased pan. Top with buffalo chicken. Sprinkle on some cheese. Finish off by drizzling on some Ranch dressing. Bake at 350 degrees for 20-25 min until dip is melted and bubbling. Serve with chips or veggies for dipping!!
