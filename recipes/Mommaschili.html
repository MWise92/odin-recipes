Momma's Chili

Description

Momma's Chili is a hearty chili dish with ranch style beans and ground beef that is sure to warm your heart and satisfy your hunger. I enjoy cooking this in the fall and winter when it's colder, but I've also prepared it in the spring and summer months when I'm craving something extra hearty.

Ingredients

  • 3 cans ranch style beans
  • 1-2 cans tomato soup
  • 1 can rotel
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 package mild chili seasoning
  • 1 white onion
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

    Brown ground beef and drain. Mix all ingredients in large pan and bring to boil. Stir in chili seasoning and let boil for 5 more min stirring occasionally. Sprinkle cheese on top, serve, and enjoy!
